FAQ

Awesome section description goes here.

  • What topics will the guided conversations cover?

    We are currently working to choose which topics will be discussed.

    The topics will be of interest to all developers who care about writing great code regardless of experience or background. Topics we are currently considering include TDD, Microservices and Refactoring.

    We are looking for people to facilitate the guided conversations, so please get in touch if you’d like to help.

  • What programming languages or tools do I need to know to attend?

    CodeCraftConf is a proudly language-agnostic conference, where we want to share ideas with as diverse a group of developers as possible. Whether you're building websites for the latest start-up with Ruby, creating financial models for a large global bank in Java or writing a front-end app for an in-house client with .Net, we want to hear from you.

  • Where is your code of conduct?

    CodeCraft has a Code of Conduct which we require all attendees to adhere to for all events.

  • Will there be alcohol at CodeCraftConf?

    CodeCraftConf will be held in the event space of Drygate, which also contains a bar/restaurant and brewery. Drygate was chosen because of its central location, excellent value, and flexible event space. It is possible to drink on-site, but we would discourage attendees from doing so until after the conference is over. We're principally engaged in thoughtful and considered conversation and that is something that becomes more difficult with the consumption of alcohol. We trust our attendees to act reasonably and responsibly.

    However, our Code of Conduct will be enforced. If, due to alcohol or other reasons, you are unable to act within the Code of Conduct (including the provisions for respectful conversation), you will be asked to leave. We do this to protect the enjoyment and safety of our attendees.
